# Play_off_success_prediction__NBA
This project was intended to predict success probabilities of NBA teams based on hustler and talent performance metrics as both aspects influence a team winning on the highest level. Hustle in particular appeals to the degree of committment a team has for winning a game.

I made use of gradient boost to identify the feature importances with respect to playing minutes in a game on average as a player. This was applied for the hustle as well as the talent metrics. Afterwards average hustle and talent metrics teamwise were calculated, followed by a bayesian belief network, which predicted the playoff outcome proability expressed for the upcoming season 2025/26. I used the data from the 2024/25 season as an input assuming that small changes in players won't make that big of an impact onto a teams stats and consuquently the prediction on either making not the playoffs at all, make it or even a deep playoff run.
